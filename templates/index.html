According to the Energy Information Agency (EIA) renewable energies expected to have the largest growth over the next thrity years in comparison to the all other energy sectors. Within the renewable energies sector, wind energy is projected to have the second largest growth in energy production. Although, development is a necessity for this gorwth to reach its full potential. One of the major problems of wind energy production is that most wind turbine sites are located in remote areas of the united states. At the current state of the national grid system, nearly half of all energy production for wind turbines will be curtailed due to over capcitance of transmissions lines. One way to solve this problem is to offer small wind turbines as a product to consumers that can put them on their homes and businesses in cities where the electricity is most needed.
